About Me

Transitioning into the field of design with a focused specialization in Interaction Design and a broad understanding of Digital Media, attained through my academic pursuits at the University of Canberra, marks the initiation of my career trajectory. Beyond the confines of academia, I am deeply engaged in various local communities, seeking to immerse myself in diverse experiences and acquire industry-relevant skills.

I approach my craft with an innate passion for creativity, consistently seeking to push the boundaries and explore innovative solutions to complex problems. Motivated by a pursuit of excellence, I am committed to refining my skills and honing my craft, always striving to deliver outcomes of the highest quality.

Versatility is a cornerstone of my professional ethos; adept at collaborating within interdisciplinary teams or functioning autonomously, I am equally comfortable assuming leadership roles when the situation demands. Adaptability is not just a trait but a way of life for me, as I embrace the dynamic nature of challenges and eagerly anticipate the opportunities they present for growth and development.
